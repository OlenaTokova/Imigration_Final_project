Immigrant Visas

Family-Sponsored Immigrant Visas

IR-1: Spouse of a U.S. Citizen

Documents: Form I-130, marriage certificate, proof of citizenship, birth certificates, police certificates, medical examination, etc.

Application Process: U.S. citizen files Form I-130; if approved, NVC processes the case and schedules a visa interview at a U.S. consulate.

IR-2: Unmarried Child Under 21 Years of Age of a U.S. Citizen

Documents: Form I-130, child's birth certificate, proof of parent's citizenship, police certificates (if applicable), medical examination, etc.

Application Process: U.S. citizen files Form I-130; if approved, NVC processes the case and schedules a visa interview at a U.S. consulate.

IR-5: Parent of a U.S. Citizen Who is at Least 21 Years Old

Documents: Form I-130, birth certificate of U.S. citizen, proof of citizenship, police certificates, medical examination, etc.

Application Process: U.S. citizen files Form I-130; if approved, NVC processes the case and schedules a visa interview at a U.S. consulate.

F1: Unmarried Sons and Daughters of U.S. Citizens

Documents: Form I-130, birth certificate, proof of parent's citizenship, police certificates, medical examination, etc.

Application Process: U.S. citizen files Form I-130; if approved, NVC processes the case and schedules a visa interview at a U.S. consulate.

Employment-Based Immigrant Visas

EB-1: Priority Workers

Documents: Form I-140, proof of extraordinary ability, employment offer, labor certification (if applicable), police certificates, medical examination, etc.

Application Process: Employer or self-petitioner files Form I-140; if approved, case is processed by NVC and scheduled for a visa interview.

EB-2: Professionals with Advanced Degrees or Exceptional Ability

Documents: Form I-140, proof of advanced degree or exceptional ability, labor certification, employment offer, police certificates, medical examination, etc.

Application Process: Employer files Form I-140; if approved, case is processed by NVC and scheduled for a visa interview.

EB-3: Skilled Workers, Professionals, and Other Workers

Documents: Form I-140, proof of skills, labor certification, employment offer, police certificates, medical examination, etc.

Application Process: Employer files Form I-140; if approved, case is processed by NVC and scheduled for a visa interview.
