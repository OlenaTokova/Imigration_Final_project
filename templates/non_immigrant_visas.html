Non-Immigrant Visas

Business/Tourism

B-1: Business Visitor

Documents: Form DS-160, passport, visa fee receipt, invitation letter from U.S. business, proof of ties to home country, etc.

Application Process: Complete DS-160, schedule and attend visa interview, provide supporting documents.

B-2: Tourist Visitor

Documents: Form DS-160, passport, visa fee receipt, proof of ties to home country, travel itinerary, etc.

Application Process: Complete DS-160, schedule and attend visa interview, provide supporting documents.

Work

H-1B: Specialty Occupations

Documents: Form I-129, job offer from U.S. employer, proof of qualifications, LCA (Labor Condition Application), etc.

Application Process: Employer files Form I-129; if approved, schedule visa interview.

L-1: Intracompany Transferee

Documents: Form I-129, proof of employment with multinational company, job offer in U.S., etc.

Application Process: Employer files Form I-129; if approved, schedule visa interview.

Student/Exchange Visitor

F-1: Academic Student

Documents: Form I-20, SEVIS fee receipt, Form DS-160, passport, financial support documents, etc.

Application Process: Complete DS-160, pay SEVIS fee, schedule visa interview, provide supporting documents.

J-1: Exchange Visitor

Documents: Form DS-2019, SEVIS fee receipt, Form DS-160, passport, proof of ties to home country, etc.

Application Process: Complete DS-160, pay SEVIS fee, schedule visa interview, provide supporting documents.
